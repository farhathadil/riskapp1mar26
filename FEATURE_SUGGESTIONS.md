# Risk Register - Suggested Features

## Current Functionality
This is an **ISO 27001:2022 Risk Register** for Support & Service Management with:
- Risk registration (CRUD operations)
- Likelihood/impact assessment with residual risk calculation
- Treatment tracking with progress indicators
- Dashboard with statistics and charts
- CSV import/export
- Local storage persistence

---

## Suggested Additional Features

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Risk trend charts over time** - Histogram showing how risk ratings have changed over periods
- **Export reports to PDF** - Professional formatted reports for stakeholders
- **Risk heat map matrix visualization** - 5x5 matrix showing likelihood vs impact distribution
- **Executive summary dashboard** - KPI-focused view for non-technical stakeholders

### Risk Management
- **Risk review workflow with approval states** - Formal review cycle with required approvals
- **Automated risk aging notifications** - Alerts for risks not reviewed in X days
- **Risk history/audit trail** - Track all changes with timestamps and user attribution
- **Risk dependencies/linkages** - Connection between related risks
- **Risk scoring templates** - Custom templates for different business units/contexts

### Collaboration
- **Comments/notes on individual risks** - Discussion thread within each risk
- **@mentions for collaborators** - Tag team members in comments
- **Document attachments to risks** - Upload evidence, control documentation
- **Notification system** - Email/app reminders for upcoming due dates

### User Experience
- **Dark mode option** - Toggle between light/dark themes
- **Bulk actions** - Update status, assign owner, or close multiple risks at once
- **Keyboard shortcuts** - Quick navigation and actions (e.g., Ctrl+N for new risk)
- **Advanced search with saved filters** - Create and save custom filter combinations
- **Risk templates** - Pre-defined risk categories for quick entry

### Compliance
- **Control effectiveness testing framework** - Test and track control performance
- **ISO 27001 compliance checklist** - Direct mapping to ISO controls
- **Generate audit reports** - Pre-formatted reports for auditors
- **Risk sign-off/approval workflow** - Electronic signatures and approvals

### Data Management
- **Cloud/backend sync** - Sync data to external database (currently local-only)
- **User authentication and role-based access** - Security with different permission levels
- **Data backup/restore** - Scheduled backups and manual restore points
- **Risk archive for closed items** - Separate view for completed/cancelled risks
